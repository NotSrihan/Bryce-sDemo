CS360 Final Project by Bryce Comer

This project implements a first-person character controller in Three.js by combining and adapting ideas from tanami-coding's character controller example and SimonDev's first-person camera tutorial.

The demo includes a fully explorable scene with a skybox, PBR materials, lighting, shadows, fog, and randomized stone archways.
